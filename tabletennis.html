TableTennis

A Pong style table tennis game. There is skill involved in aiming the shot of the ball and reacting to high speeds. This game includes difficulty adjustment, customization for the size of bumper and colour of ball. Map selection is possible but not implemented. There are cheats as in ball-tracking and aim-assist. Mouse-tracking for debug can be turned on using the 'X' button. The game gets progressively harder on each game-tick and every ball contact with the bumper. The top velocity throughout the game will be displayed in the post-game screen.


Press the X key during the game to turn on mouse tracking
